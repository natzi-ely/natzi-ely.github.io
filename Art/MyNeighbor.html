My Neighbor Totoro となりのトトロ

In this animated film, filmmaker Hayao Miyazaki follows student Satsuke and her younger sister Mei as they move in with their father and wait in an ancient rural house while their mother heals in a nearby hospital. The sisters meet and become friends with playful spirits in their home and the neighboring forest as they explore their new surroundings, most notably the enormous, cuddly animal known as Totoro. This film was released on July 13, 1990.
